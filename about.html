About Great Ethiopian Run

Great Ethiopian Run Plc. is an event management company specializing in mass-participation running events.Since its inception, Great Ethiopian Run has staged over 100 races in different parts of Ethiopia. In recent years our focus has been on taking mass-participation races to all corners of Ethiopia; developing capacity in the office and event teams that are staffed by Ethiopians; and working with sponsors on a range of publicity campaigns.
